Postseason

Tournaments

When the regular season wraps, the real fun begins. Champions are crowned across three signature tournaments every summer.

LNP Tournament

The county's marquee summer event

Dates
June 22 – July 3, 2026
Single-elimination bracket
10U · 12U · 14U

Presented in partnership with LNP | LancasterOnline, this is the league's premier postseason tournament. The best teams from each section compete for the county crown under the lights at Penn Medicine Park.

LCYBL All-Star Tournament

Best of the best from every club

Dates
July 11 – July 19, 2026
Pool play into bracket
10U · 12U · 14U

Each member club nominates its standout players to form all-star rosters. A celebration of the season's top talent, the All-Star Tournament showcases the future of Lancaster County baseball.

Club Tournaments

Hosted by member organizations

Dates
Various weekends, June – August
Invitational / round-robin
Varies by host

Throughout the summer, member clubs host their own invitational tournaments. These events welcome teams from across the region and are a great way to log extra games and build team chemistry.
