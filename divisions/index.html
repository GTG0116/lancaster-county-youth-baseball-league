Where You Play

Age Divisions

Every player finds the right level. Each division has its own field dimensions, rules and developmental focus across three balanced sections.

10U

Ages 9–10

Section 1Section 2Section 3

The entry point to LCYBL travel baseball. 10U focuses on fundamentals, fair playing time, and falling in love with the game while introducing competitive divisional play.

Basepaths

65 ft basepaths

Pitching

46 ft pitching distance

  • Player-pitch with developmental pitch counts
  • Balanced sections for competitive matchups
  • Emphasis on fundamentals and sportsmanship

12U

Ages 11–12

Section 1Section 2Section 3

The classic 'Little League age' on a slightly larger diamond. 12U sharpens game IQ, pitching depth, and situational play as athletes prepare for the full-size field.

Basepaths

70 ft basepaths

Pitching

50 ft pitching distance

  • Leadoffs and stealing fully in play
  • Pitch-count rules to protect young arms
  • Feeder for the LNP and All-Star tournaments

14U

Ages 13–14

Section 1Section 2Section 3

Full-size, big-league dimensions. 14U is the proving ground where players translate years of development into high-level competition and get ready for high school ball.

Basepaths

90 ft basepaths

Pitching

60 ft 6 in pitching distance

  • Regulation 90/60.5 diamond
  • Advanced pitching and defensive strategy
  • Showcase for the next level of play
