Where You Play

Three Divisions, One League

From first travel-ball at-bats to big-league dimensions, LCYBL has a place for every age and stage.

10U

Ages 9–10

The entry point to LCYBL travel baseball. 10U focuses on fundamentals, fair playing time, and falling in love with the game while introducing competitive divisional play.

Learn more

12U

Ages 11–12

The classic 'Little League age' on a slightly larger diamond. 12U sharpens game IQ, pitching depth, and situational play as athletes prepare for the full-size field.

Learn more

14U

Ages 13–14

Full-size, big-league dimensions. 14U is the proving ground where players translate years of development into high-level competition and get ready for high school ball.

Learn more

Why LCYBL

More Than a Game — A County Tradition

For over seven decades, Lancaster County families have trusted LCYBL to deliver competitive, community-run baseball built around player development and good old-fashioned fun.

Our StoryMember Clubs
1

Community-run

LCYBL is operated entirely by volunteers — parents, coaches and former players who give their time so the next generation can play.

2

Development-focused

Balanced sections and fair-play rules mean every athlete gets meaningful innings and a real chance to grow their game.

3

Competition that matters

From divisional standings to the LNP and All-Star tournaments, there's always something to play for under the summer lights.
